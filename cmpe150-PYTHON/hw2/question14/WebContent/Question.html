Question 14:

Coprime numbers (5 points)

Please read important information first.

Description

In this problem, given an integer n, you are expected to find the numbers which are smaller than n and coprime with n.
Two numbers a and b are coprime if their greatest common divisor is 1 (in other words, no number greater than 1 can divide both a and b).

User will enter 1 input, which will be the number n. (You can assume it will always be an integer that is bigger than 1.)

Depending on the number n, you need to print:

  • The amount of numbers which are smaller than n and coprime with n.
