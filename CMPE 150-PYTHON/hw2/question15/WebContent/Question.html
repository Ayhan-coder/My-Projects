Question 15:

Let's draw a frame (5 points)

Please read important information first.

Description

In this problem, you are expected to draw a square frame with 1 s and 0 s depending on a given side length n.

User will enter 1 input, which will be the side length of the drawn square. You can assume it will be a valid integer bigger than 2.

Depending on the side length, you need to print:

  • 1 s at the edges and corners of the square.
  • 0 s for the parts inside the square.
